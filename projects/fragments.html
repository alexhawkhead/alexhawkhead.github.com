Fragments

I designed this poster series for a typographic university paper, which followed the 2012 ISTD brief ‘It Happened on This Day.’

The series describes the first time I had a nightmare, in which I had 3 in succession the same night, that continued to plague me throughout the rest of my childhood.

The first poster in each pair tells the story of the nightmare and then the next poster is the voice of my mother consoling me. The viewer may or may not take the visual cues to construct the pairs, as long as they connect the posters together in their mind.
