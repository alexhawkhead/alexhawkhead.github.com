Illustration, copywriting, graphic design, typography.

This university brief was a way for us to explore macro and microaesthetics, by designing a booklet which unfolded to reveal a large poster.

I designed a booklet which debates whether it's better to join the good guys or the bad guys in the Star Wars universe. The points had to be clearly distinguishable between each side and the body copy played a large role in persuading the audience.

I chose to illustrate my points, both positive, for the dark side & the good side in the universe of Star Wars, using humour to aid pursuasion and comprehension.
