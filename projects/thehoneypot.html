The honey pot

This was designed for a university packaging design paper.

The brief asked me to create a 3D package which reflected my understanding of colouration (more specifically camouflage) and how it affected a particular species in its natural environment. I explored defensive strategies in nature and from that came the idea of prohibiting a user from getting to a product, thus deterring them from wanting the product at all.
